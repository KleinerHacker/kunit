KElectricCurrentUnit

enum KElectricCurrentUnit : KUnit, Enum<KElectricCurrentUnit>

The units of the electric current group.

The group is one-dimensional (no exponent-specialized subtypes) and its base unit is AMPERE (baseValue 1.0). Every baseValue is the factor to convert into the group's base unit (BASE, ampere): 1 unit = baseValue * ampere.

Besides the SI ampere the group offers the two classic CGS current units: the BIOT (abampere) of the electromagnetic system (1 Bi = 10 A) and the STATAMPERE of the electrostatic system (1 statA ≈ 3.335 641 × 10⁻¹⁰ A).

Example:

KElectricCurrentUnit.BIOT.baseValue // 10.0 (1 Bi = 10 A)
