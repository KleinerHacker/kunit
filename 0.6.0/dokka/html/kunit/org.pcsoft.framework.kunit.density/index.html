enum KDensityUnit : KUnit, Enum<KDensityUnit>

The base-unit marker of the (mass-)density group (a constructed quantity: mass · length⁻³).

KDensityUnitInstance
Link copied to clipboard
class KDensityUnitInstance : KUnitInstance<KDensityUnitInstance> , KUnitMeasurable

Wraps a KMixedUnitInstance representing a (mass-)density, i.e. exactly two terms - KMassUnit.BASE (gram) at exponent +1 and KDistanceUnit.BASE (meter) at exponent -3 (g·m⁻³). The value is the raw component value; readings in kg/m³ (KDensityUnit.BASE) divide by KGM3_IN_BASE.

Converts this mixed unit to a "pure" density value, as long as it consists of exactly two terms: one KMassUnit term at exponent +1 and one KDistanceUnit term at exponent -3 (order independent). The terms are normalized to their component base units.
